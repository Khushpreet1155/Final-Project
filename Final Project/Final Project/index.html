Our Vision

Nourishing Communities, where no one goes hungry

We at Food Banks Canada are committed to ensuring that no one in our country goes hungry. Despite living in Canada, approximately 2 million individuals rely on food banks each month.


That is why we tirelessly push for effective measures to address hunger and its underlying causes. Our ultimate goal is to eliminate the need for food banks completely.


Until we reach that goal, we collaborate with over 4,750 food banks and community organizations around the country to support and promote their important work. We make use of research to discover the primary causes of food insecurity and then act on that information.


We think that every Canadian can help end hunger.


We are the national network of food banks in Canada.


The goal of our organization is to alleviate hunger today while preventing it tomorrow.


Together with our network, we aim for a hunger-free Canada.
